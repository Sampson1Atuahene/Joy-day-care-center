About Joy Day Care Center

Mission: To provide safe, loving, and high-quality 24-hour childcare that supports children’s development and empowers families to thrive.

Vision: To become the leading 24-hour childcare provider in the community, known for excellence in care, safety, and early childhood education.

Core Values

  • Safety: Children’s health and protection always come first.
  • Joy: A positive, nurturing environment where children feel happy and secure.
  • Respect: Inclusive care for all children and families.
  • Reliability: Consistent, dependable childcare services.
  • Professionalism: Trained staff and continuous improvement.

Operations & Staffing

Open 24/7 with a director, lead teachers, overnight staff, and support personnel. All staff are background checked and trained in CPR and First Aid.
