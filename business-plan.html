Joy Day Care Center — Business Plan

Executive Summary

Joy Day Care Center is a licensed 24-hour childcare facility dedicated to providing safe, reliable, and nurturing care for children from 6 weeks to 12 years of age. The center is designed to meet the needs of working parents, particularly those with non-traditional work schedules.

Services

Infant care, toddler care, preschool, school-age, overnight and weekend care, homework assistance, meals and snacks, and educational activities.
